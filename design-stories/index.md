## [SSA.gov Digital-First Redesign](/design-stories/ssa-digital-first-redesign/)

**Enterprise Web • Human-Centered Design • Design System • 180M+ visitors/year**

A multi-year digital transformation of SSA.gov, focused on top tasks, accessibility, and design system governance.  
**Highlights:** +22.3 CSAT increase (47.7 → 70.0), 25% growth in online transactions, 550% increase in online benefit verification letters, design system in Figma aligned with USWDS.

## [SSN Card Request Tool](/design-stories/ssn-card-request-tool/)

**Workflow Design • Eligibility Logic • Content + UI**

An online tool that guides people through the process of getting or replacing a Social Security card based on their specific circumstances (name changes, citizenship, living abroad, housing instability, and more).  
**Highlights:** Complex branching logic, tailored content responses, and reduced in-office and call-center demand.
